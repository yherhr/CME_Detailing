WHAT'S INVOLVED IN A PROFESSIONAL COATING?

A proper coating job isn't just "wipe on and walk away". The result depends entirely on preparation and controlled application. Our full coating process includes:

  • Deep wash & chemical decontamination — removing road film, tar, iron, and fallout
  • Mechanical clay bar — stripping bonded contaminants for a perfectly clean surface
  • Machine polishing / paint correction as needed — levels the clear coat for maximum bond & gloss
  • Panel wipe — removes all oils and residues so the coating can chemically bond
  • Coating application & curing — applied in controlled passes, left to cure to form a hard sacrificial layer
  • Post-cure aftercare guidance — how to wash and maintain properly so the coating lasts its full life

BENEFITS OF CERAMIC COATING

  • Extreme gloss & depth — wet, glassy finish that stays looking freshly detailed
  • UV & heat resistance — prevents fading, chalking, and clear coat failure
  • Chemical protection — resists bird droppings, bug guts, tree sap, hard water, and brake dust
  • Hydrophobic "easy-clean" surface — dirt and mud don't stick; washes take a fraction of the time
  • Long-term durability — years of protection, not weeks like waxes or cheap ceramic sprays
  • Preserves value — keeps the paint OEM-fresh and improves resale appeal
