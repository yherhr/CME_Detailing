WHAT'S INCLUDED?

Our interior detail is a comprehensive deep-clean of every surface inside your vehicle — using safe, professional-grade products that are tough on dirt but gentle on your surfaces:

  • Full vacuum — carpets, seats, boot, door pockets, vents, and all crevices
  • Steam cleaning — sanitises and deep-cleans fabric, carpet, and hard-to-reach areas
  • Leather conditioning — cleaned, nourished, and protected to prevent cracking
  • Fabric & carpet stain removal — targeted treatment of spots and stains
  • Dashboard & console clean — all plastics, screens, and surfaces cleaned and dressed
  • Door cards & trim — thorough clean of all interior panels
  • Glass clean (interior) — streak-free windows and mirrors
  • Odour elimination — safe deodorising for a fresh, clean-smelling cabin
