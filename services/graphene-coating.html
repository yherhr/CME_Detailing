What is Graphene?

Graphene is a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice — the strongest material ever tested, yet incredibly thin and flexible. When incorporated into coating technology, it delivers remarkable heat dissipation, hardness, and hydrophobic performance that surpasses traditional ceramic coatings in several key areas.

BENEFITS OF GRAPHENE COATING

  • Superior heat dissipation — reduces door panel and panel temperatures, minimising water spot risk
  • Extreme hydrophobic performance — water beads and sheets off at an even steeper angle than ceramic
  • Anti-static properties — repels dust and airborne particles more effectively
  • Ultra-slick surface — the most lubricious coating available, making washing safer and faster
  • Outstanding UV protection — guards against fading, chalking, and clear coat failure
  • Long-term durability — years of protection with proper maintenance
  • Preserves OEM finish — maintains factory paint depth, clarity, and gloss

THE APPLICATION PROCESS

Like all professional coatings, graphene requires thorough preparation to bond correctly and perform at its best. Our process is identical to ceramic coating application:

  • Deep wash & chemical decontamination
  • Clay bar treatment to remove bonded surface contamination
  • Paint correction / machine polish as required
  • Panel wipe to remove all residues before application
  • Controlled graphene coating application in panels
  • Cure period and final inspection
  • Aftercare guidance included
