Why We Choose Fusion PPF™

Fusion Plus™ represents the next generation of paint protection film — combining self-healing polyurethane, UV resistance, and an optically clear finish to protect your paintwork from real-world damage. Designed for high performance and visual perfection, Fusion PPF offers invisible defence against stone chips, scratches, road debris, and harsh weather, all while maintaining a flawless showroom gloss.

Unlike cheaper films that yellow, crack, or peel, Fusion PPF is chemically stable, UV-resistant, and engineered to self-heal when exposed to heat.

WHAT'S INVOLVED IN A PROFESSIONAL PPF INSTALL?

Installing paint protection film is a precision process that requires the right environment, tools, and expertise. Our team follows strict preparation and installation standards to guarantee a perfect, bubble-free finish every time.

  • Thorough wash & decontamination — removes waxes, tar, iron, and residues for optimal film adhesion
  • Paint inspection & correction (if required) — levels the surface to eliminate swirl marks or light defects
  • Precision cutting & pattern fitting — Fusion film is computer-cut for each vehicle using digital templates
  • Application & squeegee technique — installed under controlled conditions for perfect alignment and clarity
  • Edge wrapping & finishing — ensures seamless coverage with minimal visible edges
  • Final inspection & cure — checked under lighting for perfection, then left to cure before delivery
  • Aftercare guidance — how to wash and maintain your film for maximum life and clarity

BENEFITS OF FUSION PPF

  • Self-healing top coat — light scratches and swirls vanish with heat or sunlight
  • Impact & chip resistance — guards against stone chips, debris, and insect acids
  • Hydrophobic surface — repels dirt, mud, and water for easier cleaning
  • UV & chemical protection — resists fading, yellowing, and environmental damage
  • Optically clear finish — enhances gloss while preserving OEM colour and clarity
  • Long-term durability — backed by industry-leading warranties and proven performance
