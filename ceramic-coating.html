WHAT'S INCLUDED?

Our exterior detail is a thorough, multi-step process designed to safely clean, decontaminate, and protect your paintwork:

  • Snow Job foam pre-wash — loosens surface contamination without touching the paint
  • Two-bucket safe wash method — pH-neutral soap with clean wash mitts only
  • Iron fallout & tar removal — chemical decontamination to remove embedded particles
  • Clay bar treatment — strips bonded contaminants for a smooth, glass-like surface
  • Paint correction / machine polishing (where included) — removes swirl marks and haze
  • Panel dressing — tyres, trims, and exterior plastics restored and protected
  • Glass clean — streak-free windows inside and out
  • Paint sealant or wax application — protection and enhanced gloss

BENEFITS OF PROFESSIONAL EXTERIOR DETAILING

  • Enhanced gloss & depth — restores that freshly polished, reflective finish
  • Removes contamination — eliminates tar, iron, and fallout that damage paint over time
  • UV protection — protects from fading and oxidation caused by Australia's harsh sun
  • Hydrophobic protection — water, mud, and grime slide off easily for faster cleaning
  • Safe, swirl-free process — soft wash mitts, pH-neutral soaps, and clean microfibres only
  • Preserves resale value — keeps paintwork looking newer for longer
